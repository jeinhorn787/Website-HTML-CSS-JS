Welcome To DubLand!

So you may have heard of dubstep before but don't know much about it ("That guy Skrillex created it right?"). Or maybe you have literally not the slightest idea what it is. But maybe some of you know exactly what it is and are here to look around. Well you are all in luck!


DubLand will tell you all you need to know about dubstep, such as the history of dubstep and some of the inner workings of creating dubstep. The world changed forever once music was starting to be created digitally, meaning little to no live instruments were used in production. Electronic Dance Music, or EDM, eventually spawned from this and swept the globe in so many forms, dubstep being just one of the very many.


So have a look around the site and dive deeper into dubstep. The genre is ever changing, being pushed to new and exciting boundaries each and every day. Go look into some of the hottest artists in the dubstep scene, a lot of which are still very active even though they may have been around since the beginning! Hopefully you leave this site with your head nodding a bit, it's all about the good beats and deep bass here!
